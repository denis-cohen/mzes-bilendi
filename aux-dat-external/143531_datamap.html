Welcome to the MZES employee survey!Thank you for devoting your time to complete this survey. The aim of the survey is to collect your feedback on workplace satisfaction, preferences regarding office use and the distribution of working time between remote and on-site work, career plans, experiences at work, and wishes and suggestions for further improvements of the work life at the MZES.The survey results will help the equal opportunities officers and employee representatives at the MZES to better represent your needs and interests. For example, after the first MZES employee survey in 2022, a working group was formed at the MZES to evaluate the results of the survey and develop measures for improvement.The survey is conducted by the survey institute Bilendi, which operates in accordance with the provisions of the EU General Data Protection Regulation and all other data protection regulations applicable in Germany.To ensure the anonymity of your answers, the individual data will remain with Bilendi. Neither members of the project team nor other MZES employees will have access to the individual data. Bilendi has provided us with the survey participation links, which we in turn have sent to you via email. This ensures that Bilendi cannot match the e-mail addresses to the participation links and the project team cannot match your answers to the individual survey links.Bilendi will produce a report of the results according to our requirements. Quantitative data are transmitted only in the form of aggregated distributions, either across all respondents or by groups of at least five respondents. The free-text responses are anonymised and randomly sorted by Bilendi and will be checked again by two people from the project team for implicitly de-anonymising content. In cases in which they identify a risk of de-anonymisation, they will further anonymise these contents or exclude the respective free-text responses from the evaluation. We will not receive free-text responses together with personal individual characteristics at any time.The project team submits the report to the MZES Executive Board only after it has been finally checked for potentially de-anonymising content. A second results report will then be prepared for the MZES employees, the MZES Supervisory Board, and the MZES Scientific Advisory Board. In this report, no individual free-text responses will be reproduced, neither verbatim nor in spirit. In addition, a short report is prepared for the Staff Council (Personalrat) and the Department of Equal Opportunity and Social Diversity (Stabsstelle Gleichsstellung und soziale Vielfalt). This report will consist of a brief summary of the most important results only; individual free-text responses will not be included in the report, neither verbatim nor in spirit.Please note that you will need the individualised link in the invitation email to the survey to make use of your right of withdrawal. Further information on data protection Art. 13 GDPR can be found at: LinkYou can skip any question you do not wish to answer, and you can end your participation at any time without consequences.
